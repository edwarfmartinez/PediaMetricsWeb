Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

PediaMetrics ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the PediaMetrics mobile app and this website.

1. Information We Collect

PediaMetrics is designed to work without requiring user accounts. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to our own servers through the app.

If you contact us directly by email, we receive the information you include in your message (such as your email address and message content) so we can respond.

2. Health and Measurement Data

Pediatric measurements entered in the app are processed on-device. We do not sell health data and do not use it for advertising.

3. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • No in-app ads are displayed.
  • No sale of user data.
  • No unnecessary cross-app tracking.

4. Third-Party Services and Links

The app and website may link to third-party services (for example, the Apple App Store). Those services have their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect app data on our servers, we do not retain app-submitted personal or health records. Email communications are retained only as needed to provide support and comply with legal obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

PediaMetrics is a professional health tool intended for adults, including healthcare professionals and parents/caregivers. It is not directed to children as independent users.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact
