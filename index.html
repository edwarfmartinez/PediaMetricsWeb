What PediaMetrics Does

PediaMetrics calculates and interprets WHO-based Z-scores and percentiles for key pediatric growth indicators.

Weight-for-Age (WFA) Height-for-Age (HFA) Weight-for-Length / Height (WFL/H) BMI-for-Age Head Circumference-for-Age (HCFA)

Every result includes

  • Z-score
  • Percentile
  • Clinical interpretation: severe deficit, below normal range, within normal range, above normal range, or severe excess

This removes ambiguity and eliminates manual chart interpretation.

Built for Clinical Accuracy

  • Uses WHO 2006 and WHO 2007 growth references.
  • Automatically switches between days and months when required.
  • Correctly applies age cutoffs for children under and over 5 years.
  • Centralized calculations for deterministic and consistent outputs.
  • Validated to align with clinical expectations, not loose approximations.
